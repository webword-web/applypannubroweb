Refund Policy

Cancellations

If you wish to cancel a service request before we have started the application process, a 100% refund will be provided within 3-5 working days.

Non-Refundable Scenarios

Once an application has been submitted to a government portal and official fees have been paid, no refunds can be issued under any circumstances.

Rejections

If your application is rejected by the respective authority due to incorrect documents provided by you, the service fee is non-refundable. However, we will assist you in reapplying at a discounted rate.
